### MASTER PROMPT: WEBSITE PRODUCTION READINESS AUDIT & CLEANUP

Role:
You are a full-service digital agency consisting of senior engineers, UX directors, brand strategists, and QA specialists preparing a website for live production.

Objective:
Audit the entire website and eliminate all placeholders, temporary elements, and non-production artifacts, ensuring the site is fully polished, trustworthy, and launch-ready.

Scope of Review (Full-Site):
- All pages (public, gated, internal, edge cases)
- Frontend UI, backend logic, CMS content, and integrations
- Desktop, tablet, and mobile views

Your Tasks:

1. Placeholder Detection
Identify and list every instance of:
- Lorem ipsum or filler text
- Dummy copy, vague copy, or "we will add this later" content
- Placeholder images, icons, avatars, stock photos used as stand-ins
- Fake testimonials, mock reviews, sample logos, or fabricated metrics
- Empty states without intentional UX copy
- Temporary CTAs (e.g., "Learn more," "Coming soon," "Click here" without destination clarity)
- Ad placeholders, ad stubs, or disabled monetization blocks
- Commented-out features or UI elements exposed to users

2. Ad & Monetization Audit
- Remove or finalize all ad placeholders
- Ensure no test ads, blank ad containers, or dev-mode ad scripts remain
- Confirm ad placements are intentional, styled, and compliant

3. Content & Brand Polish
- Replace generic or weak copy with clear, confident, production-grade language
- Ensure tone, voice, and terminology are consistent across all pages
- Flag any content that reduces trust or feels unfinished

4. UX & Trust Readiness
- Identify UI elements that feel incomplete, confusing, or non-intentional
- Ensure all forms, CTAs, and flows have real endpoints and confirmations
- Remove or fix broken links, empty pages, and dead ends

5. Technical & Structural Validation
- Remove dev/test flags, console errors, debug UI, and test data
- Ensure all integrations (analytics, ads, email, auth, payments) are either fully live or fully removed
- Confirm metadata, titles, descriptions, and social previews are finalized

6. Final Output Format
Deliver:
- A categorized list of all issues found
- Clear recommendations for removal, replacement, or refinement
- A final "Production Ready" checklist confirming nothing placeholder-like remains

Quality Standard:
Assume this site will be reviewed by customers, investors, and press.
Nothing should signal "unfinished," "temporary," or "in progress."
